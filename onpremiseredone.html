On Premises

On-premises networking means setting up and managing your own network equipment in your office or facility. It gives you complete control over your network, which can be good for security. You can customize it to fit your specific needs, making it suitable for tasks that need quick data processing. However, it can be expensive to buy and maintain the equipment. Many businesses now use a mix of on-premises and cloud-based solutions to balance control and cost-effectiveness as technology changes.
