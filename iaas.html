Infrastructure as a Service

Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) is a cloud computing model that simplifies IT infrastructure management. With IaaS, you don't need to buy or maintain physical servers and networking equipment. Instead, you rent these resources from a cloud provider over the internet. It's like leasing a fully equipped data center without the hassle of ownership.

IaaS offers several advantages. It allows for flexibility and scalability, as you can easily scale your resources up or down based on your needs. You also benefit from cost savings, as you only pay for what you use. Plus, IaaS providers handle hardware maintenance and security, reducing your administrative burden.

In a nutshell, IaaS is a cloud service where you rent infrastructure like servers and networks from a provider, making it a cost-effective and hassle-free solution for businesses.
